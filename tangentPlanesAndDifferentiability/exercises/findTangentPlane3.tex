\begin{exercise}

Suppose that $f(x,y)$ is differentiable and $\grad{f}(1,2) = \vector{1,3}$.  If $f(1,2)=4$, find the equation of the tangent plane to the surface $z=f(x,y)$ at the point $(1,2,4)$.

\[
x + \answer{3} \cdot y + \answer{-1} \cdot z = \answer{3}
\]

\end{exercise}
