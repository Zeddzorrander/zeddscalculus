\begin{exercise}

Suppose $\{a_n\}_{n=1}$ is a sequence defined by the formula:

\[a_n = 4+n \cos(n \pi) \]

Write down $a_1$, $a_2$, $a_3$, $a_4$, and $a_5$.

    \begin{align*}
      a_1 &= \answer[given]{3} & 
      a_2 &= \answer[given]{6} & 
      a_3 &= \answer[given]{1} & 
      a_4 &= \answer[given]{8} & 
      a_5 &= \answer[given]{-1} 
    \end{align*}

\begin{exercise}    
Thus, the rule above generates the ordered list:

\[
\answer{3} ,\answer{6},\answer{1},\answer{8},\answer{-1}, \dots
\]
